$$\begin{eqnarray}
条件: \quad
&①& \quad f(x) \in \mathbb{K}[x] 
\end{eqnarray}$$
$$if \quad \deg \ f(x) =n $$
$$\quad \Updownarrow \quad$$
$$f(x)= \sum\limits_{i=0}^{n}a_{i}x^{n-i}$$
$$\quad \Downarrow \quad $$
$$f^{'}(x) = \sum\limits_{i=0}^{n-1}(n-i)a_{i}x^{n-i-1}$$
$$\quad \Downarrow \quad $$
$$f^{'}(x)  \ \  \overset{\triangle}{=} \ \ f(x)的\textcolor{orange}{\underline{\textbf{一阶形式微商}}}$$
